9 June

Berlin

A couple of years ago we hosted the first RabbitMQ Summit ever in London, with visitors from all over the globe. Last year we repeated the success. And this summer, Erlang Solutions and 84codes (CloudAMQP) invites you to the third edition of RabbitMQ Summit - in Germany, Berlin.

We hope to see our friends again and that a lot of new friends will join us, to take part together in the constant evolution of message-queuing and RabbitMQ.
